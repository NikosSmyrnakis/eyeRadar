Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 2025

Introduction

EyeRadar is a research utility app that captures eye-tracking data during reading sessions. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Data Collection

  • User Information: Name, grade, ID, email, and date of birth (if entered by you).
  • Reading Sessions: Eye-tracking data and session logs collected during reading tasks.
  • Authentication: EyeRadar uses Sign in with Apple for login. Apple may share your name and email if you choose.

Data Use

We use collected data solely for research purposes and to provide app functionality. We do not sell or share data with third parties.

Storage

All session data is stored locally on your device. Exporting or sharing this data is done only when you choose to share it.

Data Sharing

When you use the export feature, reading sessions are packaged into ZIP files and shared through the iOS share sheet. You control when and how your data is shared.

Your Rights

You may delete the app at any time, which removes all locally stored data. For questions about your data, contact us at nick8smirn@gmail.com.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
