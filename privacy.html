Face Data (TrueDepth/ARKit) – App Review Summary

What face data does the app collect?
EyeRadar uses ARKit on supported iPhone devices to access eye-tracking–related signals during a session:
  • Gaze vectors & lookAtPoint (derived from ARKit face anchors)
  • Eye transforms (left/right eye pose matrices) and blink openness estimates
  • Timestamp & session metadata (frame time, device model, iOS version)
Not collected / not stored Raw RGB camera frames, depth maps, and full face geometry/mesh are not saved or transmitted.
Planned uses of the collected face data
Collected signals are used solely to enable gaze-based reading research features:
  • Place a moving dot/selector and log where the user looks on the text
  • Compute reading metrics (e.g., fixation counts, dwell time, saccade jumps)
  • Local on-device UI adaptation (e.g., optional calibration)
Never used for user identification, authentication, advertising, or profiling.
Will face data be shared with third parties? Where is it stored?
No automatic sharing. Data remain on device storage by default. Sharing can occur only if you explicitly export via the iOS share sheet (e.g., Mail, Files, AirDrop). We do not run any non-Apple backend servers.
How long is face data retained?
Ephemeral (processing-only)
Per-frame ARKit inputs (camera/depth/mesh) are processed in memory and discarded immediately.
Session logs
Derived gaze samples and metrics you choose to save are kept until you delete them or uninstall the app.

Introduction

EyeRadar is a research utility app that captures eye-tracking–based interaction signals during reading sessions. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Data Collection (only locally, nothing is shared automatically)

  • User Information: Name, grade, ID, email, and date of birth (only if you enter them).
  • Authentication: Sign in with Apple. Apple may provide your name and/or relay email depending on your choice.
  • Reading Sessions
    • Gaze vectors, lookAtPoint , timestamps
    • Optional calibration parameters

We do not collect background location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or analytics tied to your identity.

Face Data (TrueDepth/ARKit)

What we process

On iPhones that support face tracking, EyeRadar uses ARKit to access eye-tracking–related signals during active sessions: gaze vectors and lookAtPoint, left/right eye transforms, and blink openness estimates. These signals are processed on device to position cursors and compute reading metrics.

What we do not store

  • No saving of RGB camera frames
  • No saving of depth maps or TrueDepth raw data
  • No saving of full face mesh geometry

How we use these signals

Solely to enable reading-research features (e.g., gaze-controlled navigation, fixation timing) and optional calibration. Not used for user identification, authentication, advertising, marketing, or profiling.

Sharing

We do not share face data with third parties. Derived session logs remain on your device unless you export them via the iOS share sheet that you control.

Storage & Security

Per-frame ARKit inputs are handled in volatile memory and discarded immediately after processing. If you save a session, only derived gaze samples/metrics and calibration values are written to local device storage. We do not operate any non-Apple backend.

Retention

Ephemeral ARKit inputs are not retained. Saved session logs persist on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.

Data Use (General)

We use collected data solely to provide app functionality and conduct reading-related research features that you invoke. We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

Storage & Export

All session data are stored locally on your device. Export happens only when you choose to share via the iOS share sheet (e.g., Mail, Files, AirDrop). Exported files (e.g., ZIP/CSV) contain only derived session data and optional calibration parameters.

Sign in with Apple

EyeRadar supports Apple’s authentication flow. Depending on your choice, Apple may share your name and a relay email address. We use this information only to create and maintain your app account and do not share it with third parties.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Export: You choose when and how to share exported files.
  • Delete: Delete sessions in-app or uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.
  • Questions: Contact nick8smirn@gmail.com.
