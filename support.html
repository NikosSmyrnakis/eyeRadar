Requirements

  • iPhone with TrueDepth front camera (Face ID models).
  • iOS 18 or later.
  • Sign in with Apple for authentication.

How to Use EyeRadar

  1. Sign in with Apple when prompted.
  2. Choose a text from the list and follow the on‑screen instructions to begin a reading session.
  3. Complete the brief AR calibration when requested to enable accurate eye tracking.
  4. After finishing, go to the Explorer tab to view your sessions.
  5. Select one or more sessions and use Share to export ZIP files via the iOS share sheet.

Troubleshooting

Camera/AR not starting
Ensure camera permission is granted in Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera.

Face not detected
Use a well‑lit environment and keep the device about arm’s length from your face.

Share not showing apps
Save to Files first, then attach the ZIP from Files to your preferred app.

Data & Privacy

EyeRadar stores reading sessions on your device. Exporting/sharing is initiated by you. For details, see our Privacy Policy.
